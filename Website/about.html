Secrets of the Deep

The main objective in the game is to take a photo of "The Golden Fish",
but it has sinked to the bottom of the ocean.
On the way there you will meet 3 types of fish:

1. Normal fish - They are moving in a straight line.
You will lose a heart if you walk into them.

2. Hostile fish - If you get too close to them,
they will start chasing you.

3. Special fish - They work like normal fish,
but you can take a photo to get points.

The mechanics of the game are:

1. Moving - WASD or Arrows

2. Taking photos - Left Mouse Button (LMB)

3. Flashlight - Right Mouse Button (RMB)
